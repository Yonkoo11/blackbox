# Blackbox — Demo Video Script

**MONEY SHOT (Gate 2, build first):** the live verifier catching a tamper — toggle "flip a byte" on
a stored record, the seal recomputed in the browser stops matching the on-chain seal, that record and
every one after it flip VERIFIED → TAMPERED. This single live transition proves the product.

**Medium:** Motion mode — Playwright records the real live verifier (https://yonkoo11.github.io/blackbox/)
and drives the tamper toggle. No static slideshow.

**Voice:** ElevenLabs Brian (Starter, commercial license). Phonetic: write "Sui" as "Swee" in the audio
script only. No em-dashes. Contractions. ≤15 words/sentence. Credit "Voice: ElevenLabs" in description.

## Clip 1 — hero + inclusion proof  (visual: verifier loaded, VERIFIED verdict + inclusion-proof line)
This is an AI agent's wallet on Swee. It spends real money, and every move is sealed on chain. Here's the proof, recomputed live in your browser.

## Clip 2 — guardrail  (visual: scroll to the on-chain guardrail card)
The agent can't go rogue. Its spending limit, rate cap, and allowed recipients are enforced by the contract, not by trust.

## Clip 3 — record tape  (visual: the linked record tape, entries VALID)
Every action becomes a tamper evident memory. Encrypted with Seal, stored on Walrus, chained on chain.

## Clip 4 — money shot setup  (visual: cursor moves to a record's "flip a byte" toggle)
So what if someone edits one stored record? Flip a single byte, and watch.

## Clip 5 — money shot payoff  (visual: toggle ON, TAMPERED cascade, recomputed seal turns red, no match)
The seal no longer matches. That record and every one after it turn red. The chain caught it.

## Clip 6 — close + CTA  (visual: toggle back OFF, VERIFIED restored, hold on verifier + URL)
Blackbox. Give an AI agent money without trusting it. Verify any agent yourself, link below.
